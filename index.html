Federal Sock Reclamation Bureau

A Solemn Duty to Every Sock

Millions of socks go missing every year in the nation. They are lost in the laundry, abandoned in changing rooms, or fall from bags unnoticed. For too long, this crisis has been ignored.

In accordance with the "National Laundry & Hosiery Act of 1988", the Federal Sock Reclamation Bureau (FSRB) is the sole government body authorized to investigate, track, and resolve cases of missing individual socks. We are dedicated to bringing order to the chaos of laundry day and reuniting soles across the nation.
